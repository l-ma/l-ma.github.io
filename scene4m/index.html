Scene4M: A Multimodal Scene Model

Lara Marinov, Christian Egeland, Anthonin Duval, Kenza Driss

Scene4M is a foundation model designed to understand and generate scenes across multiple modalities, including video, audio, and text. It is an extension of the existing 4M model. More details are in our full report. This website shows video and audio clips that could not be added in our paper.
